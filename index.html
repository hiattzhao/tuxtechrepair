Why We Exist

The E-Waste Problem

Millions of usable computers are discarded every year, contributing to a massive global e-waste crisis.

The Digital Divide

Meanwhile, countless students and families lack access to the digital tools necessary for education and employment.

Our Solution

We bridge this gap by refurbishing donated hardware with open-source software and placing it where it matters.

What We Do

Expert Repair

Our volunteer technicians professionally diagnose, clean, and repair donated hardware. We prioritize data security with military-grade wiping.

Community Donation

Restored devices are distributed to local schools, non-profits, and families in need, completely free of charge.

Join the Movement

Whether you have old tech to donate, skills to share, or simply want to support the cause, there is a place for you at TuxTechRepair.
